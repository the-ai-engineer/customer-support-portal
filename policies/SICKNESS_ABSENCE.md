# Sickness Absence Policy

## Purpose

This policy explains what employees should do when they are unwell and unable to work.

## Reporting Sickness

Employees must tell their line manager as soon as possible on the first day of sickness absence.

The update should include the expected length of absence, if known, and any urgent work that needs to be covered.

Employees should contact their manager by phone or message before 10:00 unless there is an emergency.

## Short-Term Absence

For sickness absence of up to seven calendar days, employees may self-certify their absence when they return to work.

Managers may hold a return-to-work conversation after any sickness absence.

## Longer Absence

If sickness absence lasts more than seven calendar days, the employee must provide a fit note from a doctor or qualified healthcare professional.

Employees on longer absence should keep their manager updated at agreed intervals.

HR may support the employee and manager with adjustments, phased returns, or occupational health advice.

## Sick Pay

Company sick pay may be available depending on length of service and the circumstances of the absence.

Questions about sick pay should be directed to HR.

## Questions

Questions about sickness absence should be sent to hr@example.com.
