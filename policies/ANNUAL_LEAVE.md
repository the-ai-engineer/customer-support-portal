# Annual Leave Policy

## Purpose

This policy explains how employees can request and use annual leave.

## Entitlement

Full-time employees receive 25 days of paid annual leave each holiday year, plus public holidays.

Part-time employees receive a pro-rata annual leave allowance based on their contracted working hours.

The holiday year runs from 1 January to 31 December.

## Requesting Leave

Employees should request annual leave through the company HR system.

Requests should normally be submitted at least two weeks before the first day of leave.

Managers review requests based on team coverage, business needs, and fairness across the team.

## Carrying Leave Forward

Employees may carry forward up to 5 unused days into the next holiday year with manager approval.

Carried-forward leave must normally be used by 31 March of the following year.

## Restrictions

Employees should not book travel before their leave request is approved.

More than 10 consecutive working days of annual leave requires approval from both the line manager and HR.

## Questions

Questions about annual leave should be sent to the HR team at hr@example.com.
