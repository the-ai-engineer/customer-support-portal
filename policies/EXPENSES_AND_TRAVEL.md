# Expenses and Travel Policy

## Purpose

This policy explains how employees should book business travel and claim work-related expenses.

## Approval

Employees must get manager approval before making significant work-related purchases or booking business travel.

Expenses may be rejected if they are not reasonable, not work-related, or not approved in advance where approval is required.

## Submitting Claims

Expense claims should be submitted through the finance system within 30 days of the purchase.

Employees must provide receipts for all expense claims unless Finance confirms an exception.

Claims should include the business reason for the expense.

## Travel

Employees should choose cost-effective travel options that are appropriate for the business need.

Standard class rail travel should be used unless a manager approves an exception.

Flights should only be booked when they are necessary and approved by the line manager.

## Meals

Reasonable meal costs during approved business travel may be claimed.

Alcohol cannot be claimed unless it is part of an approved customer or company event.

## Personal Expenses

Personal items, commuting costs, fines, and entertainment that has not been approved are not reimbursable.

## Questions

Questions about expenses and travel should be sent to finance@example.com.
