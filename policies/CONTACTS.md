# HR and Workplace Contacts

## Purpose

This document lists useful internal contacts for HR policy questions.

## HR Team

General HR policy questions should be sent to hr@example.com.

Employees can contact HR about annual leave, sickness absence, parental leave, workplace adjustments, and remote working.

## Finance Team

Expense and travel questions should be sent to finance@example.com.

Finance can help with expense claims, missing receipts, rejected claims, and reimbursement timelines.

## Line Managers

Employees should speak to their line manager about day-to-day approvals, workload coverage, team schedules, and planned absence.

## Confidential Concerns

Employees with confidential workplace concerns may contact HR directly.

If an employee has an urgent safety concern, they should contact their manager or the office manager immediately.
