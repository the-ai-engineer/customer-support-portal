# Parental Leave Policy

## Purpose

This policy summarises leave options available to employees who are parents or are becoming parents.

## Maternity Leave

Eligible employees may take up to 52 weeks of maternity leave.

Employees should tell HR and their manager about the pregnancy and expected week of childbirth by the end of the 15th week before the baby is due, where possible.

## Paternity Leave

Eligible employees may take up to 2 weeks of paternity leave following the birth or adoption of a child.

Paternity leave should normally be taken within 56 days of the birth or adoption placement.

## Adoption Leave

Eligible employees adopting a child may take up to 52 weeks of adoption leave.

Employees should notify HR after receiving confirmation of an adoption match.

## Shared Parental Leave

Eligible parents may be able to share leave and pay through shared parental leave.

Shared parental leave arrangements must be discussed with HR before leave dates are confirmed.

## Keeping In Touch

Employees on parental leave may agree reasonable keeping-in-touch days with their manager.

Keeping-in-touch days are optional and must be agreed in advance.

## Questions

Questions about parental leave should be sent to hr@example.com.
