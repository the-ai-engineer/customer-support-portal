# Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-25

Northstar Outfitters accepts returns for most physical products within 30 days of delivery. Customers may request a refund, exchange, or store credit when the item meets the conditions in this policy.

This policy applies to online orders placed through the Northstar Outfitters website. It does not apply to marketplace orders, wholesale orders, or products bought from third-party retailers.

## Eligible Returns

Items are eligible for return when:

- the return is requested within 30 days of delivery
- the item is unused or only lightly inspected
- the item is returned with original packaging where possible
- the item includes all accessories, manuals, labels, and components
- the customer can provide the order number, receipt, or account email address

Opened items may be returned if they are complete, undamaged, and have only been inspected in a normal way. For example, a customer may open a backpack, check the pockets, and try the straps. A customer may not return a backpack that has been used on a trip unless it is faulty.

Items with minor packaging damage may still be returned if the product itself is in returnable condition.

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned unless faulty:

- final sale items
- digital gift cards
- personalized or custom-made products
- hygiene-sensitive items once the seal has been broken
- replacement parts sold as final sale
- products damaged by misuse, neglect, or unauthorized repair

If an item arrived damaged, faulty, or incorrect, the customer should contact support instead of starting a standard return.

## Starting A Return

Customers can start a return from their account order history or by contacting support with their order number.

Support may ask for photos when the item is damaged, faulty, missing parts, or different from what the customer ordered.

Returns must be posted within 14 days after the return request is approved. If the item is not posted within that window, the return approval may expire and the customer may need to submit a new request.

## Refund Timing

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 to 10 business days after the returned item is received and inspected.

Store credit can usually be issued faster, within 2 business days after inspection.

Banks, card issuers, and payment providers may take additional time to show the refund in the customer's account. Northstar Outfitters cannot speed up bank processing times after the refund has been issued.

## Return Shipping

Customers pay return shipping unless the item arrived damaged, faulty, or incorrect.

If the store made an error, support will provide a prepaid return label.

Customers are responsible for packaging returned items securely. Northstar Outfitters may reduce or refuse a refund if the item is damaged during return shipping because it was not packed safely.

## Exchanges

Customers can request an exchange for a different size, color, or replacement item when stock is available.

If the replacement item costs more, the customer must pay the difference. If it costs less, the difference is refunded.

If the requested replacement is out of stock, support may offer a refund or store credit instead.

## Partial Refunds

Northstar Outfitters may offer a partial refund when:

- an item is returned with missing accessories
- the product has avoidable damage
- packaging is missing for a product where packaging is part of the item
- the customer agrees to keep a product with a minor cosmetic issue

Support must explain the reason for a partial refund before it is issued.

## Gifts

Gift recipients may receive store credit when they can provide the order number or the purchaser's email address.

Refunds to the original payment method can only be issued to the original purchaser.

## Contact

Customers with refund questions should contact support with their order number and a brief description of the issue.
