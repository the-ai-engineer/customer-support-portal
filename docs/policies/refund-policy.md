# Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-25

Customers can return most items within 30 days of delivery for a refund, exchange, or store credit.

## Eligible Returns

Items are eligible for return when:

- the return is requested within 30 days of delivery
- the item is unused or lightly inspected
- the item is returned with original packaging where possible
- the customer can provide the order number or receipt

Opened items may be returned if they are complete, undamaged, and include all accessories.

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned unless faulty:

- final sale items
- digital gift cards
- personalized or custom-made products
- hygiene-sensitive items once unsealed

## Refund Timing

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 to 10 business days after the returned item is inspected.

Store credit can usually be issued faster, within 2 business days after inspection.

## Return Shipping

Customers pay return shipping unless the item arrived damaged, faulty, or incorrect.

If the store made an error, support will provide a prepaid return label.

## Exchanges

Customers can request an exchange for a different size, color, or replacement item when stock is available.

If the replacement item costs more, the customer must pay the difference. If it costs less, the difference is refunded.
