# Shipping Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-25

Northstar Outfitters ships online orders to customers in the United Kingdom, European Union, United States, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand.

Shipping availability may vary by product, warehouse location, carrier restrictions, and local delivery rules.

## Processing Time

Orders are usually processed within 1 to 2 business days.

Orders placed after 2pm local warehouse time are processed the next business day.

Orders are not processed on weekends or public holidays.

During major sales, product launches, or severe weather disruptions, processing may take up to 4 business days. If processing is delayed beyond 4 business days, customers should receive an email update.

## Delivery Options

Standard shipping usually takes:

- United Kingdom: 2 to 4 business days
- European Union: 4 to 8 business days
- United States and Canada: 5 to 10 business days
- Australia and New Zealand: 7 to 12 business days

Express shipping is available in selected regions and usually takes 1 to 3 business days after dispatch.

Delivery estimates are counted from dispatch, not from the time the order is placed.

Express shipping does not skip normal order processing time unless the checkout page explicitly says priority processing is included.

## Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are shown at checkout before the customer places the order.

Free standard shipping may be available when the order meets the current free-shipping threshold shown at checkout.

Customs duties, import taxes, or local handling fees may apply to international orders. These charges are the customer's responsibility unless checkout explicitly states that duties and taxes are included.

## Tracking

Customers receive a tracking link by email once the order has shipped.

Tracking can take up to 24 hours to update after the courier receives the package.

If an order ships in multiple parcels, the customer may receive more than one tracking link.

Customers with an account can also view tracking from their order history.

## Lost Packages

An order is considered potentially lost when tracking has not updated for 7 business days after dispatch.

Customers should contact support with their order number if they believe a package is lost.

If the courier confirms the package is lost, the store will offer a replacement or refund.

Support may ask the customer to confirm the shipping address, check with neighbours, and check safe-place delivery locations before a lost-package claim is opened.

If tracking shows the order was delivered but the customer cannot find it, support may open a courier investigation. Courier investigations usually take 3 to 7 business days.

## Address Changes

Customers can request an address change before the order is dispatched.

After dispatch, the store cannot guarantee address changes and the customer may need to contact the courier directly.

Northstar Outfitters is not responsible for failed delivery when the customer entered an incorrect address and the order had already been dispatched before the customer requested a correction.

## Failed Delivery And Returns To Sender

If a courier returns an order because delivery failed, support will contact the customer when the parcel arrives back at the warehouse.

The customer may choose reshipment or refund when the item is returned in saleable condition.

Additional shipping charges may apply when failed delivery was caused by an incorrect address, missed delivery attempts, or refusal of delivery.

## Split Shipments

Some orders may ship from more than one warehouse. Split shipments do not change the total shipping cost paid at checkout.

Items in a split shipment may arrive on different days.

## Contact

Customers with shipping questions should contact support with their order number and tracking link, if available.
