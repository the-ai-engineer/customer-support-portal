# Account Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-25

Customers can shop with Northstar Outfitters as guests or create an account to manage orders, addresses, saved preferences, and support requests.

An account is optional for placing most orders, but some features are only available to account holders.

## Account Creation

Customers can create an account using an email address and password.

Customers must use an email address they can access because order updates and password reset links are sent by email.

Customers are responsible for keeping their login details secure and for updating their email address if it changes.

Northstar Outfitters may block account creation or close an account when it appears to be used for fraud, abuse, payment disputes, or policy violations.

## Password Reset

Customers can reset their password from the login page.

Password reset links expire after 30 minutes.

Support cannot see or share a customer's password.

If a customer cannot receive the reset email, they should check spam filters, confirm they are using the correct email address, and then contact support.

Support may ask identity-check questions before helping with account access.

## Order History

Account holders can view recent order history, shipping status, and saved addresses.

Guest checkout orders are not automatically shown in account history unless the same email address is used and the customer verifies ownership.

Order history may not show marketplace orders, wholesale orders, or purchases made through third-party retailers.

Support can help customers find an order when they provide the order number, shipping postcode or ZIP code, and account email address.

## Billing Profiles

Customers can save billing and shipping addresses in their account.

The store does not store full card numbers. Payment details are handled by the payment provider.

Saved addresses can be edited or removed from the account area.

Removing a saved address does not change the shipping address for an order that has already been placed. Customers must contact support quickly if they need to correct an order address before dispatch.

## Guest Checkout

Guest checkout customers receive order updates by email.

Guest customers can still request returns, refunds, warranty help, and privacy support by contacting support with their order number.

Creating an account after guest checkout does not always import older orders automatically.

## Account Deletion

Customers can request account deletion by contacting support from the email address linked to the account.

Account deletion removes saved addresses, preferences, and login access.

Some order records may be retained where required for tax, fraud prevention, chargeback handling, or legal compliance.

Account deletion does not cancel active orders, open returns, warranty claims, or unresolved payment issues.

Customers who want to delete personal data should also review the privacy policy, because some deletion requests are handled under privacy rules rather than account rules.

## Account Security

Customers should contact support immediately if they believe someone else has accessed their account.

Support may temporarily lock an account while investigating suspicious activity.

Customers should not share passwords or password reset links with support staff or anyone else.

## Contact

Customers with account questions should contact support from the email address linked to the account when possible.
