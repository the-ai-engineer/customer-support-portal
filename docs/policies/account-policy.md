# Account Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-25

Customers can shop as guests or create an account to manage orders, addresses, saved preferences, and support requests.

## Account Creation

Customers can create an account using an email address and password.

Customers must use an email address they can access because order updates and password reset links are sent by email.

## Password Reset

Customers can reset their password from the login page.

Password reset links expire after 30 minutes.

Support cannot see or share a customer's password.

## Order History

Account holders can view recent order history, shipping status, and saved addresses.

Guest checkout orders are not automatically shown in account history unless the same email address is used and the customer verifies ownership.

## Billing Profiles

Customers can save billing and shipping addresses in their account.

The store does not store full card numbers. Payment details are handled by the payment provider.

## Account Deletion

Customers can request account deletion by contacting support from the email address linked to the account.

Account deletion removes saved addresses, preferences, and login access.

Some order records may be retained where required for tax, fraud prevention, chargeback handling, or legal compliance.
