# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-25

Northstar Outfitters collects only the personal information needed to process orders, provide support, prevent fraud, meet legal obligations, and improve the shopping experience.

This policy summarises how customer data is handled for the course project. It is fictional and not legal advice.

## Data Collected

The store may collect:

- name
- email address
- shipping and billing address
- order history
- customer support messages
- device and browser information for security and analytics

The store does not store full payment card numbers.

Payment card details are processed by a payment provider. Northstar Outfitters may receive limited payment information such as payment status, card brand, expiry month, expiry year, and the last four digits of the card.

The store may also collect return details, warranty claim evidence, communication preferences, fraud-risk signals, and delivery instructions.

## How Data Is Used

Customer data is used to:

- process and deliver orders
- send order and shipping updates
- provide customer support
- handle returns, refunds, and warranty claims
- prevent fraud and abuse
- comply with legal obligations

Customer data may also be used to:

- investigate lost packages, failed deliveries, refunds, and chargebacks
- verify account ownership when support is contacted
- send service messages about orders, returns, warranties, or account security
- improve website reliability and customer support quality

## Data Sharing

Customer data may be shared with service providers needed to run the store, including payment processors, shipping carriers, support tools, and analytics providers.

The store does not sell customer personal data.

Service providers may only use customer data to provide services to Northstar Outfitters, unless the customer has a separate relationship with that provider.

Customer data may be shared when required by law, regulation, legal process, fraud prevention, or payment dispute handling.

## Data Retention

Order records are usually retained for 7 years for accounting, tax, fraud prevention, and legal purposes.

Support messages are usually retained for 3 years unless a longer retention period is required for an active dispute.

Account profile data is retained while the account remains open.

Return and warranty records are usually retained for 3 years after the case is closed.

Marketing preference records may be retained to make sure customers who opt out are not contacted again by mistake.

## Deletion Requests

Customers can request deletion of personal data by contacting support.

Some records may be retained when legally required, including order, tax, fraud prevention, and chargeback records.

Deletion requests do not automatically cancel active orders, refunds, warranty claims, or support cases.

When data cannot be deleted immediately, support should explain the reason and the expected retention period where possible.

## Marketing Preferences

Customers can unsubscribe from marketing emails using the unsubscribe link in the email or by contacting support.

Customers may still receive service emails about orders, delivery, returns, warranty claims, account security, and privacy requests.

## Security

Northstar Outfitters uses reasonable security measures to protect customer data.

Customers should use a strong password and should not share account credentials, password reset links, or one-time verification codes.

If a customer believes their account or personal data has been compromised, they should contact support immediately.

## Contact

Customers with privacy questions or deletion requests should contact support from the email address linked to their account or order.
