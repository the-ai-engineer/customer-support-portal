# Warranty Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-25

Most physical products sold by Northstar Outfitters include a 12-month limited warranty from the date of delivery.

The warranty is intended to cover manufacturing defects and product failures that occur during normal use. It is not a general damage protection plan.

Some products may include a different warranty period when the product page clearly states different terms.

## What Is Covered

The warranty covers manufacturing defects, faulty materials, and failures that occur during normal use.

Examples of covered issues include:

- product stops working during normal use
- seams, fasteners, or parts fail unexpectedly
- electrical components fail without misuse
- product arrives with a manufacturing fault

The warranty may also cover missing parts when the customer reports the issue within 30 days of delivery.

## What Is Not Covered

The warranty does not cover:

- accidental damage
- cosmetic wear and tear
- misuse or neglect
- water damage unless the product is advertised as water-resistant
- damage from unauthorized repair or modification
- lost or stolen items
- consumable parts that naturally wear out over time
- damage caused by using the product outside its intended purpose
- normal fading, stretching, scuffing, or small scratches from regular use

The warranty does not cover problems caused by third-party accessories, modifications, or repairs.

## Warranty Claims

Customers should contact support with:

- order number
- description of the issue
- photos or video showing the problem
- confirmation of when the issue started

Support may offer troubleshooting steps before approving a replacement or repair.

Customers should not dispose of the product before the claim is resolved unless support confirms in writing that disposal is allowed.

If support requests the product for inspection, the customer must send the product within 14 days of receiving return instructions.

## Remedies

If a warranty claim is approved, the store may offer:

- replacement part
- full product replacement
- repair where available
- store credit if the item is discontinued

Refunds are only offered when replacement, repair, or store credit is not reasonable.

Replacement products may be new or refurbished, but they must be equivalent in function and condition.

Warranty replacement does not restart the original 12-month warranty period. The replacement remains covered for the longer of 90 days or the remaining time on the original warranty.

## Proof Of Purchase

Customers normally need an order number, receipt, or account email address to make a warranty claim.

If the product was received as a gift, support may still help when the customer can provide the original order number or purchaser's email address.

Products bought from third-party retailers are not covered by Northstar Outfitters' direct warranty process. Customers should contact the retailer where they bought the product.

## Relationship To Returns

Warranty claims are separate from standard returns.

If a customer wants to return a working item within 30 days, the refund policy applies.

If a product fails because of a covered defect, the warranty policy applies even after the 30-day return window has closed, as long as the warranty period is still active.

## Contact

Customers with warranty questions should contact support with the order number, a description of the fault, and photos or video of the issue.
